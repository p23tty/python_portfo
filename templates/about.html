03 : About me

Work path

I work as a data engineer at LENSA - which is a prominent job board platform in the United States - contributing to the success of the company's data-driven operations. My responsibilities are:

  • Design and implement AWS CDK infrastructure to automate data ingestion and processing
  • Creating data sets for analytics in Athena and Redshift
  • Identify, design and implement internal process improvements
  • Solve challenging data integrations problems, utilizing optimal ETL patterns, frameworks, query techniques, sourcing from structured and unstructured data sources

Started my career in BI area in Lisbon, Portugal where I worked for Chassis Brakes through ERASMUS programme. I was doing work both on databases and creating dashboards for end users from different data sources, eg.: sql, SAP, APIs.

Then I had the opportunity to join Druck.at which is an online printing company based in Austria where I worked as a data engineer. I was working closely with departments related to finance, marketing and production. My tasks were to migrate data from legacy database to Snowflake (cloud based datawarehouse), to create/maintain dashboards in Tableau/Looker, and support business decisions with data.

I gained valuable experience as a Data Solution Engineer at Emarsys. My responsibilities included creating and maintaining ETL jobs using Python and Airflow for seamless data extraction, transformation, and loading. I also worked on Emarsys' data product, utilizing MySQL for effective data management. I successfully migrated data from on-premise to cloud-based databases, leveraging my expertise in cloud technologies. Collaborating with stakeholders, I provided valuable input in scoping projects to align with business objectives. Working with BigQuery, I facilitated data storage, retrieval, and analysis, enabling advanced analytics and reporting. Using the pandas library, I transformed data and created exports to support data-driven decision-making processes.
