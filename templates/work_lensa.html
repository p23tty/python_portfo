Data Solution Engineer

At LENSA, a leading job board platform in the United States, I serve as a Data Engineer, contributing to the success of the company's data-driven operations. In this role, my responsibilities included leveraging my expertise in data engineering to support various aspects of Lensa's platform. As a Data Engineer, I played a vital role in ensuring the seamless flow of data within Lensa's infrastructure. My work required to design and implement scalable data pipelines, enabling efficient data ingestion, processing, and storage. By optimizing data workflows, I helped enhance the platform's performance and reliability, ensuring a smooth experience for both job seekers and employers. My responsibilities are:

  • Design and implement AWS CDK infrastructure to automate data ingestion and processing
  • Creating data sets for analytics in Athena and Redshift
  • Identify, design and implement internal process improvements
  • Solve challenging data integrations problems, utilizing optimal ETL patterns, frameworks, query techniques, sourcing from structured and unstructured data sources
